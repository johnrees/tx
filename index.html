Tierra Extrania Barcelona

Philosophy

Tierra Extraña is a vertical gardening project that celebrates new creative ways to reconnect with Nature.

Finding playful ways to reintroduce plants into our homes is a way to bring us closer to where we used to belong. Tierra Extraña attempts a shift from a short-sighted use & throw to a longer run mutually beneficial relationship with Nature. This initiative nurtures interconnectedness, balance and respect for the next one and the wholeness that we make up together.

Extrañar is Spanish for longing – a central concept to this project. Tierra Extraña longs for a stronger and more interconnected Earth – Home to all of us. The dream is to restore the lost link and return to a harmonic communion with Mother Nature.

Separation is an illusion - we are all in this together.
